About Us

Hot-Rod Scooters is a veteran and family-owned business that was founded on April 12, 1999. That's about a quarter-century of scooter building experience! We are happy to serve everyone and provide you all with a scooter that can help you and your families have some fun or establish those core memories together. We are very customer oriented, and we would love to hear feedback from you on what we can do to make are scooters better. Please consider emailing us at support@hotrodscooters.com.
